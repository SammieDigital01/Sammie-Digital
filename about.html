ABOUT SAMMIEDIGITAL

An agency built around one belief: a website is a system, not a brochure.

We started SammieDigital to close the gap between "looks nice" and "makes money," and we've stayed stubborn about it ever since.

OUR STORY

It started with one frustrating pattern.

Client after client came to us with the same story: a beautiful website that nobody could update, a funnel that leaked leads at every step, or a CRM full of dead automations nobody trusted. The sites looked fine. They just didn't work.

SammieDigital was built to fix that pattern: treating design, funnel logic, and CRM automation as one connected system instead of three separate vendors handing off blame. What began as freelance web builds for local businesses has grown into a full-service digital agency with a team spanning design, development, copy, and automation, serving coaches, clinics, law firms, and growing brands across Nigeria and abroad.

OUR JOURNEY

From freelance builds to a full systems practice.

YEAR ONE

Freelance foundations

Started building Shopify and WordPress sites for local businesses, learning firsthand what makes a small-business owner trust (or abandon) a website.

YEAR TWO

Beyond the website

Clients started asking for more than a homepage: landing pages, cart logic, and automation. SammieDigital expanded into funnels and CRM builds.

YEAR THREE

Systems-first, by name

Formalized the agency around one principle: design, funnel, and automation as one connected system, and took on law firms, clinics, and engineering firms.

TODAY

A full agency team

SammieDigital has grown from a one-person practice into a full team: design, development, copy, automation, and marketing, serving clients across Nigeria and internationally.
